Extra Muros is an itinerant creative residency program that aims to bring together innovative and creative artists from all around the globe for a two-week long production and writing camp.

It is held every year in professional recording studios across changing locations and countries based on FLEE's prior research and work.

Each edition of the residency gives birth to of a collaborative music compilation, a filmed documentary, as well as a series of live events.

Extra Muros aims to create the framework of an honest and modern intercultural discourse and exchange by breaking codes and boundaries in an inclusive and hybrid manner with young underground artists from all over the world.
