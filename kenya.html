In late August 2019, the very first edition of Extra Muros was held at the Elefant Studio in Nairobi, Kenya. For two weeks, the participating artists shared their lives, artistic practices and toured through Kenya and Uganda presenting their work at various venues in Nairobi, Lamu, Kilifi, Mombasa and at the Nyege Nyege Festival (Uganda)
