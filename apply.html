Eligibility



Artists will be awarded residencies based on their current activity as an artist, their previous body of work and their proposal for new work.
We do not accept students or enlisted scholars
We do accept collectives (apply via a single application)
Previous participants and ALUMNI to Extra Muros residencies are not eligible.



Selection Process



A jury of experts, past residents, and Extra Muros staff will convene to consider candidates.
We will be accepting up to six residents in total for every edition which will all be notified per email/phone in due time.
