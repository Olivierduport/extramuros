Extra Muros is an itinerant creative residency program that aims to bring together innovative and creative artists from all around the globe for a two-week long production and writing camp.
Artists
Apply
In late August 2019, the very first edition of Extra Muros was held at the Elefant Studio in Nairobi, Kenya.
For two weeks, the participating artists shared their lives, artistic practices and toured through Kenya and Uganda presenting their work at various venues in Nairobi, Lamu, Kilifi, Mombasa and at the Nyege Nyege Festival.
